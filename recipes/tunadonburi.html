Tuna Donburi 鉄火丼

Tuna donburi or tekkadon is a Japanese rice bowl topped with tuna sashimi that has been marinated in a savory or sweet mixture of sake, soy sauce and mirin. This dish was commonly served from the end of the Edo period to the beginning of the Meiji period in Japan.

Ingredients

  • Sashimi grade tuna (maguro)
  • Rice
  • Green onions or scallions
  • Toasted white sesame seeds
  • Shredded nori seaweed (kizami nori)
  • Shiso leaves (perilla/ooba)
  • Soy sauce
  • Mirin
  • Sake

Steps

  1. Marinate the tuna with the seasonings (soy sauce, mirin, sake) for 3-5 min.
  2. Place the tuna on top of steamed rice that has been cooled down for 2-3 min
  3. Sprinkle chopped scallions, shredded nori seaweed, roasted white sesame seeds, and julienned shiso leaves.
