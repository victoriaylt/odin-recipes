Dakgalbi 닭갈비

Dakgalb is spicy stir-fried chicken made by stir-frying marinated diced chicken in a gochujang-based sauce with sweet potatoes, cabbage, perilla leaves, scallions, tteok, and other ingredients. This dish originated from Chuncheon in the Gangwon province in Korea.

Main Ingredients

  • Chicken thigh fillets
  • Sweet potatoes
  • Carrots
  • Cabbages
  • Korean perilla leaves
  • Korean rice cakes (tteokbokki)

Marinade Sauce Ingredients

  • Korean chili paste (gochujang)
  • Korean chili flakes (gochugaru)
  • Korean curry powder (karegaru)
  • Rice wine
  • Soy sauce
  • Sugar
  • Black pepper
  • Minced garlic
  • Minced ginger

Steps

  1. Combine all the marinade ingredients in a bowl and mix. Pour the sauce over the chopped chicken and marinate for at least 30 min.
  2. Preheat a large skillet on medium high heat and add cooking oil. Place all vegetables and rice cakes into the skillet and add meat on top. Cook on medium high heat for 3-4 min.
  3. Reduce heat to medium to medium low and cook further until all is cooked (about 10 - 15 min)
