Bún Riêu

Bun riêu is a popular, flavor-packed Vietnamese rice noodle soup characterized by its vibrant red color and spoonfuls of crab paste mixed with eggs. This noodle soup is filled with a rich meat broth, fish sauce, tofu, tomatoes, and shrimp paste.

Ingredients

  • Pork
  • Crab paste
  • Dried shrimp
  • Eggs
  • Chicken broth
  • Fish sauce
  • Onions
  • Roma tomatoes
  • Vermicelli noodles

Steps

  1. Par boil the pork in a large pot. Once the water boils, take out the pork and set it aside. Pour out the water and put the pork back into the pot and cover it with fresh water.
  2. Add onions, dried shrimp, and salt. Bring the water to a boil, reduce to low heat, and simmer the soup for an hour.
  3. While the soup simmers, cook green onions and chili powder with oil in a pan for 10 min.
  4. After the soup simmers for an hour, add tomatoes and the mixture from the pan. Simmer for 30 min.
  5. Whisk eggs and crab paste. After the soup simmers for 30 min, add the mixture, chicken broth, and fish sauce.
  6. Once the egg and crab mixture floats to the top of the soup, the soup should be done (10-15 min).
